# Evaluation Framework

This document outlines the evaluation framework for assessing the performance of retrieval and generation systems.

## 1. Metrics for Retrieval Quality
- **Precision**: The ratio of relevant documents retrieved to the total documents retrieved.
- **Recall**: The ratio of relevant documents retrieved to the total relevant documents.
- **F1 Score**: The harmonic mean of precision and recall.
- **Mean Average Precision (MAP)**: Computes the average precision across all relevant documents for a query.
- **Normalized Discounted Cumulative Gain (NDCG)**: Measures the quality of rankings by considering the position of relevant documents.

## 2. Metrics for Generation Accuracy
- **BLEU Score**: Evaluates the quality of text generated by comparing it to human reference text.
- **ROUGE Score**: Measures the overlap of n-grams between the generated text and reference text.
- **METEOR Score**: Considers synonyms and stemming in its evaluation for a more nuanced scoring.
- **Human Evaluation**: Qualitative assessments by human judges focusing on fluency, relevance, and informativeness.
